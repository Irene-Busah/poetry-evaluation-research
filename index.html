How This Works

You will be shown 9 pairs of poems, presented side by side. Each pair shares the same theme.

Read both poems carefully, then answer five short questions about each pair. These questions ask about overall strength, emotional impact, originality of language, personal preference, and human authorship.

There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in your personal, considered judgment.

You must answer all questions on each page before advancing. Navigation is one-way — you cannot return to a previous comparison.

The study takes approximately 15-30 minutes to complete.
